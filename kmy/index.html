What is this ? Who is the creator ?

"Kimetsu no Yaiba," also known as "Demon Slayer," is a popular manga created by Koyoharu Gotouge. Set in Taisho-era Japan, the story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is killed by demons. Tanjiro seeks revenge for his family and aims to cure his demon sister while facing powerful demons and uncovering the secrets of their world.
